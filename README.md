##  Visualization Overview

The notebook produces several visualizations, each highlighting different dimensions of Zurichâ€™s population, housing, and income development.

---

### **1. Line Plot: Housing Stock by Construction Period**
**File:** \`Line.png\`  
Shows z-standardized trends in **rooms per apartment** and **living area per apartment** across construction periods for each district.

---

### **2. Choropleth Maps: Apartments by District**
**Files:**  
- \`Map_2008.png\`  
- \`Map_2016.png\`  
- \`Map_2024.png\`

Each map visualizes:

- Number of apartments per district  
- District labels and values  
- Small embedded bar chart  
- Total KPIs for the selected year  

---

### **3. Absolute Growth Analysis**
**File:** \`Bar_abs.png\`  
For the period **2020â€“2024**, compares the absolute increase in:

- Total apartments  
- Total living area  
- Total population  

Three aligned bar charts provide an intuitive comparison.

---

### **4. Relative Growth Shares + Deviations**
**File:** \`Bar_rel.png\`

Two vertically stacked charts:

1. **Relative shares** (%) of each district in the citywide total  
2. **Difference** in percentage points relative to the apartment share  

Helps identify districts that grew â€œmore in people than in housingâ€ or vice versa.

---

### **5. Waffle Charts: Demographic Composition**
**File:** \`Waffle.png\`

For each district (2020â€“2024):

- % Children  
- % Retirees  
- % Rest of population  

Represented in a 10Ã—10 waffle grid per district.

---

### **6. Bubble Chart: Income vs Population**
**File:** \`Bubble.png\`

Encodes for each district-year:

- x-axis: median taxable income  
- y-axis: total population  
- bubble size: number of apartments  
- color: district ID  
- transparency: older years vs latest year  

Reveals socioeconomic clustering and development over time.
